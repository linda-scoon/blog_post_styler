Buying on izebuy

You do not need an account to browse. Start from the home page to see the latest and featured listings, or dig into a specific area:

  • Browse by category — open the categories page to drill into vehicles, electronics, property, services and more.
  • Search & browse listings — the main listings page with the full search and filter bar.
  • Explore on the map — on pages where the discovery map is shown you can pan around to see what is for sale near a place, or use the "Browse by city" shortcuts.

Open any listing to see its photos and video, price, description, location on a map, and the seller's details. Tap the save (heart) button to keep a listing in your account for later.

When you are ready to enquire, contact the seller directly using their Call or WhatsApp button, or the "Ask a question" form on the listing. That message goes to the seller — izebuy is not part of the conversation, and we do not handle payment or delivery. Arrange those directly, and always safely (see below).

Searching, filtering & sorting

The filter bar sits above every listings grid. Type a keyword to search, then narrow the results with any combination of filters:

  • Category — limit results to one category.
  • Location — search any place in Malawi (or tap "Use my location") and set a distance radius to see only listings around there.
  • Price — show only Discounted items, "Contact for price" listings, or Free items.
  • Currency — type a currency (for example MWK for Malawian Kwacha, or USD) to show listings priced in it.
  • Date — restrict to a date range.

Then choose how to sort. Featured, Newest and Oldest are always available. Two more sorts unlock when you give them what they need:

  • Price: low to high / high to low — choose a Currency first. Listings can be priced in different currencies, so price sorting only makes sense within one currency at a time.
  • Nearest — set a Location first, then sort by distance to see the closest listings at the top.

Your selections apply instantly, and the results keep loading as you scroll.

Selling on izebuy

  1. Create a free account. Register with your email and confirm it, or sign in with Google in one tap.
  2. Post your listing. Use "Post an ad" and fill in the details. Add clear photos (and a video if you have one), choose the right category, set the location, enter a price in Malawian Kwacha or another currency — or mark it Free or "Contact for price" — and write an honest, detailed description in English or Chichewa. Good photos and a clear description sell far faster.
  3. Go live. Depending on the site's settings your listing may be quickly reviewed before it appears. Featured listings are always reviewed first.
  4. Manage it in "My Ads". Edit, save drafts, see each listing's status (Active, Pending, Draft, Expired or Off), renew, or delete — all from your account.

Listings stay live for 30 days and then expire; renewing brings them back in one click. Posting is always free — and so is featuring a listing or getting verified. If you would like either, just contact us and ask.

Sellers & storefronts

Every seller automatically gets a public storefront — a single page that gathers all of their live listings along with their contact details and location. It is a simple shopfront you can share with a link, so returning buyers can see everything you have for sale in one place.

Trusted sellers can carry a light-blue "Verified" badge next to their name on their listings and storefront. Verification is free — contact us to request it. The badge reassures buyers that you are a genuine, established seller.

Staying safe & reporting

Meet in a safe, public place, inspect the item before you pay, and never send money for goods you have not seen. If something feels wrong, walk away.

Found a problem listing or seller? Every listing and seller page has a "Report" link. Reports reach the izebuy team so we can act on them. (This is different from "Ask a question", which goes to the seller.) Reporting suspicious or prohibited listings helps keep izebuy safe for everyone.

Your account & the app

A free account lets you post and manage listings, keep saved listings, and run your storefront. You can register with an email address or sign in with Google.

izebuy also installs like an app: open it in your phone's browser and choose "Add to Home Screen". You get an app icon, a full-screen experience, and basic offline support — no app store needed.

Contacting us

For help, featuring or verification, contact us by email or on our social channels.

A note for marketers: please do not use our contact channels for marketing, SEO, advertising, site-improvement or web-design offers. We do not take those enquiries.

Frequently asked questions

Is izebuy free?

Yes — completely. Browsing, posting a listing, featuring a listing and getting a verified badge are all free. izebuy is a place to connect buyers and sellers; we never charge for it.

What languages can I use?

izebuy works in English and Chichewa. Post your listings and message sellers in either language — whichever is easier for you and your buyers.

How long does a listing stay live?

A listing stays live for 30 days, then it expires and drops out of the listings. You can renew it in one click from "My Ads" whenever you like.

How do I post a listing?

Create a free account (or sign in with Google), then choose "Post an ad". Add a few clear photos, pick the right category, set the location and price, write an honest description, and publish.

How do I get my listing featured?

Just contact us and ask — it is free. Featured listings get extra visibility around the site. Reach us by email or on our social channels.

How do I get the verified badge?

Contact us to request verification — it is free. Once approved, a light-blue "Verified" badge shows next to your name on your listings and your storefront, which helps buyers trust you.

How do I search for something specific?

Type what you want in the search box, then narrow it with the filters: Category, Location (with a distance radius), Price, Currency and Date. Combine as many as you like.

How do I sort listings by price?

Pick a Currency filter first, then choose "Price: low to high" or "high to low". Because listings can be in different currencies, price sorting only makes sense once you have chosen one to compare within.

How do I find listings near me?

Set a Location (or tap "Use my location") and optionally a radius, then sort by "Nearest". You will see the closest listings first.

How do I contact a seller?

Open the listing and use the seller's Call or WhatsApp button, or "Ask a question". Note that "Ask a question" goes straight to the seller, not to izebuy — we are not part of the conversation or the deal.

Does izebuy handle payment or delivery?

No. izebuy connects buyers and sellers; you arrange payment and collection directly between yourselves. Always meet in a safe place, inspect the item first, and never pay for something you have not seen.

How do I save listings I like?

Tap the save (heart) button on any listing. Your saved listings are kept in your account so you can come back to them.

How do I report a problem listing or seller?

Every listing and every seller page has a "Report" link — use it and the report reaches the izebuy team. (Remember: "Ask a question" goes to the seller, while "Report" comes to us.)

Can I edit, renew or delete my listing?

Yes — go to "My Ads" in your account. From there you can edit, save drafts, renew an expired listing, check its status, or delete it.

Is there an app?

Yes — open izebuy in your phone's browser and choose "Add to Home Screen". It installs like an app and even works partly offline.

What can I not post?

Anything illegal or restricted — for example stolen, counterfeit, unsafe or prohibited goods. The full list is in our Terms & Conditions; please read them before posting.

How do I contact izebuy?

Email us, or reach us on our social channels. Please note we do not take marketing, SEO, site-improvement or web-design offers through our contact channels.
