# How to Earn Money Online in Malawi

This is a list of real ways to make money online from Malawi. These are the ones that actually pay people here, with the steps to set each one up and get the money to you. No quotes, no mindset talk. If you're reading this you already know you want money, and you don't need me to convince you of that.

They're grouped by what you need to start with. Some cost nothing but time on your phone; a few need a little money up front. Begin wherever you actually are.

And one thing that trips up most people here before they even begin: earning the money online is the easy part. Getting it out of the internet and into your hand is where everyone gets stuck. So that's where we start.

## First: yes, you can actually get paid in Malawi

People will tell you that you can't get paid online in Malawi. That was more true years ago. It is not true now. Here is how the money reaches you, simplest first.

**Mobile money (Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba).** For anything you sell *locally*, to other Malawians, this is all you need. They pay you, the money is on your phone, done. No bank, no foreign anything.

**Payoneer: your master key for international work.** This is the one to set up. Payoneer works in Malawi, and it gives you something powerful: a free **global receiving account**, with actual US, UK and Euro bank account numbers in *your* name. Foreign platforms (Fiverr, Upwork, Amazon, ad networks, marketplaces) pay into that account, and you withdraw to your Malawian bank or card. Most platforms that "don't support Malawi" actually *do*, through Payoneer.
- Go to **payoneer.com** → Register → choose **"Freelancer / receive payments from marketplaces."**
- Use your real name and National ID. They verify you (photo of your ID, sometimes a selfie). This takes a day or two, so do it now and it's ready before you earn anything.
- Once approved you'll see your **Receiving Accounts** (USD, GBP, EUR). That's what you give to platforms.
- To get money to yourself: **Withdraw → to your Malawian bank account.** (Add your bank once.)

**PayPal.** Works in Malawi and some platforms (like Patreon) pay Malawians through it. It's fine as a *backup*, but Payoneer is cleaner for most things, so make Payoneer your main one.

**What does NOT work here:** **Stripe** is not available in Malawi. So if a platform says it *only* pays through Stripe, skip it. You won't get the money. (We'll point these out as we go.)

> Set up Payoneer first. Everything below assumes you have it.

## Earn this week: no money to start

These need zero capital. The first two don't even need an international payout. You get paid in mobile money, today.

### 1. Buy and resell on Facebook, WhatsApp and izebuy
The oldest business there is, moved online. You don't need stock of your own to begin.
- Find something people want and that someone is selling cheaper than the going rate: second-hand phones, clothes (bales), shoes, kitchenware, anything.
- Photograph it well (clean background, daylight, a few angles). Post it on **Facebook Marketplace**, in local **buy-and-sell Facebook groups**, on your **WhatsApp Status**, and list it on **izebuy.com**.
- When someone wants it, you arrange the sale, they pay you by **Mpamba/Airtel Money**, you deliver or meet.
- Level up: become the person who *sources* hard-to-find things. Take a deposit, buy it, deliver it. Your profit is the margin.
- **Cost:** zero. **Payout:** mobile money. **Speed:** today.

### 2. Sell a service people find online
You don't need a "skill" you paid for. You need something you can *do* that someone will pay to not do themselves.
- Examples that work locally: typing/printing documents, designing simple posters in Canva, writing CVs and applications, tutoring a school subject over WhatsApp, cleaning, deliveries, phone screen repair, braiding/barbering, baking.
- Make a simple **Facebook Page** and a **WhatsApp Business** account (free, see the tools section). Post what you do, your prices, and three good photos.
- Tell everyone. Post in local groups. Ask happy customers to refer you. This grows by word of mouth faster than anything.
- **Cost:** zero. **Payout:** mobile money. **Speed:** this week.

### 3. Online micro-tasks (training AI, small jobs)
Companies pay people to label images, check AI answers, transcribe audio, and do small tasks. The pay per task is small, but it's real money you can start earning with no experience.
- Sites that take workers from our part of the world and pay through **PayPal or Payoneer**: **Clickworker**, **Outlier** (the new home of Remotasks), **Prolific**, **Appen**.
- Sign up for **several** of them with your real details, and check each one daily. Work appears and disappears, so you rotate to wherever the tasks are.
- Connect your **Payoneer** (or PayPal) as the payout method in each.
- **Cost:** zero. **Payout:** Payoneer/PayPal. **Speed:** days. **Honest note:** this is small money for steady effort. Good for starting, not for getting rich.

## Sell your skills to the world: freelancing

This is where it gets serious, and where AI changes everything. You no longer need to be the best writer, designer or coder in the world. You need to be able to *deliver good work*, and AI helps you do that faster than you could alone.

### Fiverr and Upwork (paid through Payoneer)
- Pick one thing you can deliver: writing, simple graphic design, data entry, translation (**English ↔ Chichewa is a real, in-demand skill** most of the world can't do), virtual assistance, basic websites.
- Create an account on **Fiverr.com** (easiest to start, where you list fixed-price "gigs") and/or **Upwork.com** (you bid on jobs).
- Write a clear profile: what you do, for whom, and *proof*, even practice samples you made yourself.
- **Use AI to lift your quality and speed:** draft with ChatGPT (free) or Claude, then edit it so it's genuinely good. AI helps you write cleaner English, design with Canva's AI, and turn around work faster. Your job is to direct it and check it.
- **Getting paid:** in your account settings, set the withdrawal method to **Bank transfer (powered by Payoneer)** or your Payoneer account. Money lands in Payoneer, you withdraw to your bank.
- **Cost:** zero. **Payout:** Payoneer. **Speed:** first job in days-to-weeks; builds with good reviews.

## Build something that pays you again and again

Everything above trades your time for money. These build an *asset*: something you make once that keeps earning. They take longer to pay off, but they compound. All can be started for free.

### 1. Sell digital products and take payments: Gumroad, Ko-fi, Patreon
A digital product is anything you make once and sell forever: a CV template, a set of Canva designs, a study guide, an ebook, lesson notes, music, presets, artwork.
- **Gumroad** (gumroad.com): upload your product, set a price, share the link. Pays out by **PayPal** (and bank in some places). Works for Malawi via PayPal.
- **Ko-fi** (ko-fi.com): take tips/donations and sell products. Use the **PayPal** payout option (Ko-fi's other option, Stripe, doesn't work here, so choose PayPal).
- **Patreon** (patreon.com): monthly memberships from fans. **Patreon supports payouts to Malawi via PayPal** (and Payoneer). Good if you make something regularly: art, writing, tutorials.
- ⚠️ **Avoid Buy Me a Coffee.** It does **not** pay out to Malawi. Use Ko-fi instead; it does the same thing.
- **Cost:** zero. **Payout:** PayPal/Payoneer. **Speed:** depends on your audience.

### 2. Sell designs without holding stock: print-on-demand
You upload a design; they print it on t-shirts, mugs, phone cases, stickers and ship it to the buyer. You never touch the product or pay upfront.
- **Redbubble** and **Printful/Printify** are the main ones. You can design with free tools (Canva) or AI image tools.
- They pay by **PayPal** or into your **Payoneer** receiving account.
- **Cost:** zero. **Payout:** PayPal/Payoneer. **Speed:** slow burn. Earns as people discover your designs.

### 3. Your own website + Google AdSense (+ affiliate)
This is the classic "earn while you sleep" path: build a site about something you know, get visitors from Google, and earn from ads and affiliate links. It is a **long game**, months not days, but it's the one that can grow into real, passive income. *This is one of the methods worth following step-by-step exactly.*

**Step 1. Build the site for free with Blogger.**
- Go to **blogger.com** (it's Google's own, free, and AdSense plugs straight in). Sign in with a Google account → **Create New Blog** → pick a name and a free `.blogspot.com` address.
- (Later, when you're earning, you can buy your own domain (about **$10–15/year**, roughly MK20,000) from Namecheap. Not needed to start.)

**Step 2. Let AI help you write, but write about something real.**
- Choose a topic you genuinely know and that people search for. Local + specific wins: "renting a house in Lilongwe," "best secondary schools in Blantyre," "how to register a business in Malawi."
- Use ChatGPT (free, **chatgpt.com**) to draft. A prompt that works:
  > *"Write a detailed, honest 800-word guide on [topic] for readers in Malawi. Use simple clear English, real specific details, and a helpful tone. Break it into sections with headings."*
- **Then edit it into the truth.** AI gets facts wrong. Add real prices, real places, real experience. Google rewards genuinely useful pages and ignores thin AI filler, so the editing is the actual work.
- Publish 15–20 solid articles before expecting much. Keep going.

**Step 3. Turn on the ads (AdSense).**
- In Blogger: **Earnings → sign up for AdSense.** Google reviews your site (you need real content and a bit of traffic first).
- Once approved, ads appear automatically and you earn per view/click.
- **Getting paid:** AdSense pays once you reach **$100**. You add a payment method under **Payments → add payment method → "Wire transfer to bank account"**, and enter your Malawian bank's details (account number + SWIFT/BIC code, which your bank gives you). Google wires you US dollars. *(If wire transfer is the option shown, you're good: it's how publishers in countries without local payout get paid.)*
- **Honest note:** $100 is a real threshold and takes time at the start. This is a build-it-and-it-grows play, not money this week.

**Add affiliate income to the same site: Admitad.**
- **Admitad** (admitad.com) is an affiliate network that **accepts Malawi** and lets you promote big stores including **AliExpress**.
- Sign up, get approved, grab your special product links, and put them in relevant articles ("the exact phone I recommend → link"). When a reader buys, you earn a commission.
- Pays out at **$10+** via PayPal/wire. Combine this with your AdSense site: same traffic, two income streams.

### 4. Publish an ebook: Amazon KDP
You can publish a book on Amazon for free and earn royalties on every sale, worldwide.
- Write the book (AI can help you draft and edit, but it has to be *good* and genuinely yours). Even a focused 30–50 page practical guide sells.
- Go to **kdp.amazon.com**, create an account, upload your manuscript and a cover (design it free in Canva), set your price.
- **Getting paid:** KDP pays by **bank wire transfer** to many countries. In your KDP account, under tax/payment details, check whether Malawi is listed for wire transfer. **If it isn't, use your Payoneer global receiving account** (the US account numbers Payoneer gave you) to receive the royalties, then withdraw to your bank. That's the bridge that makes KDP work from here.
- **Cost:** zero. **Payout:** wire / Payoneer. **Speed:** earns as people find your book.

### Also worth a look (same payout rules apply)
- **Write stories** on **Wattpad** or **Tapas.** They have programs that pay writers, open to English writers, payout via PayPal. Good if writing is your thing.
- **Sell a course** on **Udemy** or **Skillshare** if you can teach a skill on video. They pay via PayPal/Payoneer.
- **Stock photos** on **Shutterstock/Adobe Stock** if you take good pictures. Payout via Payoneer/PayPal.

## What is NOT worth your time or your money

You are trying to *earn*, so don't hand your money or hours to things designed to take them. Quickly, and from experience:

- **Paid surveys.** The pay per hour is below minibus fare. They mostly make *their* money from *you* signing up. Skip them.
- **"Investment" and forex schemes that promise daily profits.** If someone guarantees you returns, it's a trap. Malawi has had a wave of these, and the authorities keep issuing warnings about them for a reason.
- **Pyramid / MLM schemes** (and "join, recruit, earn"). Only the people at the very top earn; everyone below is the product. Rampant here right now under many names. Walk away.
- **Gambling and betting.** It is not income. The house is built to win.
- **Any "job" that asks you to pay first**: registration fees, training fees, "starter kits." Real work pays *you*. If you must pay to start, it's a scam.

None of these is judgment. They're just the doors that lead nowhere, and you don't have time or money to waste on doors that lead nowhere.

## The part nobody tells you

One more thing, take it or leave it. It took me years to actually hear this, even with people telling me the whole time. The first few times it just washed over me, and it only sank in slowly, somewhere around the hundredth telling.

Look at what people spend money on. Businesses, apps, films, songs, games. None of it fell from the sky; somebody made it up. They weren't smarter than you, or luckier in some way you'll never get near. They decided what they wanted and started building before they felt ready, and didn't stop.

Money is made up too. It's paper and numbers we all agreed to take seriously, and what it really tracks is value: people hand it over when you've given them something they wanted. So the better question than "how do I get money" is "what can I make that people actually want?" Answer that one honestly and people will pay you for it.

Where you were born and what you started with decides where you begin. It doesn't decide what you can build. Pick one thing off this page and start it this week.
