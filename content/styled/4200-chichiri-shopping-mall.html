Chichiri shopping mall resides in Blantyre a few kilometres from Kamudzu stadium.

It offers renting space for various types of businesses ranging from retail shops, mobile phone companies, restaurants, banks, and many other stores.

HOW TO GET TO CHICHIRI SHOPPING MALL

You can travel to Chichiri shopping mall by minibus, taxi, or your own car.

To get to the mall by minibus from Blantyre city, you need to catch a bus from Mibawa bus rank in Blantyre heading to Limbe via Chipembele highway and get off at Chichiri shopping mall bus stop close to the stadium.

It takes approximately 15 minutes to get there and then it's only 2 minutes walk to the mall. You should expect to pay K1,000 for a return minibus fare.

If you decide to use a taxi, it would take you about 10 minutes to get there and you should expect to pay K3,000 for a return taxi fare.

There is a big parking area if you decide to use your own car but be aware that parking a car there is at your own risk.

SHOPS AND SERVICES YOU CAN FIND AT CHICHIRI SHOPPING MALL

Services

Food: Places to eat include Kips African diner and Chicken Cottage which serve chips, ice creams and other food.

Banking Services: There is a National Bank branch and FDH Bank branch but you can also find ATM's for National Bank of Malawi, NBS Bank and Standard Bank

Dentist: Hope Dental care

Entertainment: cine city cinema

Other services

  • Puma filling station, which also has an express shop

Shops

Pep store: Pep is a clothes and accessories store that sells baby clothes and utensils as well as men's, women's and children's clothes.

FINAL REVIEW OF THE CHICHIRI SHOPPING MALL

Cleanliness: The mall is also well kept and cleaned. On cleanliness I rate it 5/5
