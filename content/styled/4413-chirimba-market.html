Chirimba Market resides in Blantyre, a few kilometres from Blantyre CBD. The market serves surrounding communities of Kamedza, Mbayani and Chapima.

It's not known exactly when Chirimba market was established but locals say that it has been around for over 60 years.

HOW TO GET TO CHIRIMBA MARKET

You can travel to Chirimba Market by minibus, taxi, or your own car.

To get to the market by minibus from Blantyre CBD, you need to catch a bus from Kandodo bus stop in Blantyre heading to Chileka via M1 road and get off at Chirimba Bus stop.

It takes approximately 15 minutes to get there and then it's only a 2 minute walk to the market. You should expect to pay K600 for a return minibus fare.

If you decide to use a taxi, you should expect to pay K3,000 for return taxi fare, which takes about 10 minutes to get there.

It is not advisable to use your own car as there is no secure parking at the market.

THINGS YOU CAN BUY AT CHIRIMBA MARKET

Fresh Fruits and vegetables: Chirimba market is full of cheap fresh fruits and vegetables in different varieties. In fact, you can buy any type of fruits and vegetables there.
Sweet Potatoes: Chirimba markets also has many traders selling fresh and good quality sweet potatoes.
Live Chickens: When you think of buying cheap and good quality live chickens, think of Chirimba as it has many traders selling live chickens.
Groceries: You can buy various types of groceries at Chirimba market as it has many traders owning grocery shops.

SHOPS AND SERVICES YOU CAN FIND AT CHIRIMBA MARKET

Services

Mills: There are a few mills for milling maize, and all sorts of seeds you would like to have milled.

Barbershops: Chirimba market has many barbershops.

Salons: You can also find hair styling services at Chirimba market as it has many hair dressing salons.

Tyre Fitting: ; You can also get your vehicle tyres fitted and air refilling services at Chirimba market.

FINAL REVIEW OF THE CHIRIMBA MARKET

Price: The prices in Chirimba market are the same as Mbayani Market, very cheap and reasonable. For this I rate it 5/5 on price.

Cleanliness: The market better kept and cleaned compared to nearby Mbayani Market. I give it a rating of 3/5 for cleanliness.
